The Space

About Asali

Asali is a family entertainment center rooted in the warmth of 90s nostalgia and powered by the Claremore community. The name comes from the Swahili word for honey — sweet, natural, something people are drawn to.

We're building more than an arcade. Asali blends retro gaming, custom birthday experiences, kids cooking parties, financial literacy programming, and a local artisan showcase — all under one roof. A space where you don't just consume entertainment, you experience community.

Our hybrid nonprofit/for-profit model means the mission comes first. Community education and youth development through the nonprofit arm. Sustainable operations through experience revenue. Clean books, clear purpose.

The Founder

Jasman Said

Student. Entrepreneur. Community builder.
Based in Claremore, Oklahoma.

Jasman is a creative entrepreneur building Asali from the ground up. With deep ties to the Claremore community and a vision for what this town deserves, she's turning raw energy into a real, fundable concept — one relationship at a time.

She believes the best businesses aren't built in isolation — they're built by including the people who already make the community what it is. Every artisan, every local business, every kid who walks through the door is part of the Asali story.

This isn't just a business plan. It's a 600-day mission to build something Claremore hasn't seen before.
