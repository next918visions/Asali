Why This Works Here
No direct competition in Claremore — first mover advantage in an underserved family entertainment market
90s / Y2K nostalgia is at peak cultural demand — perfectly timed for parents who grew up in that era
Kids cooking parties are rare, Instagram-worthy, and extremely high margin — a true differentiator
Local artisan integration makes the community a stakeholder, not just a customer
Hybrid nonprofit/for-profit model unlocks grant funding while sustaining operations through experience revenue
